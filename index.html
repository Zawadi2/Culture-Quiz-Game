Culture Quiz

Gameplay:

Start the game: Click the "Start" button to begin the quiz.

Read the question: A question will appear on the screen, along with four answer options.

Choose an answer: Select one of the answer options by clicking on it.

your answer: If your answer is correct, you'll earn points and the next question will appear. If your answer is incorrect, the correct answer will be revealed, and you'll move on to the next question.

Keep playing: Continue answering questions until you've completed all the questions in the quiz.

View your results: After completing the quiz, your final score will be displayed, along with a summary of your answers.

Retake the quiz: If you want to play again, click the "Retake Quiz" button to start a new game.

Additional features:

Timer: You have a limited time (60 seconds) to answer the question. If you don't answer within the time limit, the next question will appear.

Scorekeeping: Your score is displayed at the top of the screen, and you'll earn points for each correct answer.

Question navigation: You can navigate through the questions using the "Next" buttons.
